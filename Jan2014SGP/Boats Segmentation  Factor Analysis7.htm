Step 4. Develop a scree plot and decide on the number of factors to be derived

Team Analysis: Our initial analysis of potential factory using the scree plot suggested five factors with an eigenvalue above 1 which would explain 54% of the variance. However, these five factors failed the qualitative test of providing a coherent story. We pushed from five to six and then seven to arrive at the smallest set of factors that provided a consistent story explaining 61% of the variance. We anchored our initial analysis at 5 factors with an eigen value above 1. We wanted to maintain the smallest number of factors that provided a consistent story so that we were able to easily manage the analysis and explain the results to others. The association of variables to factors is provided in section 5, table 2.
